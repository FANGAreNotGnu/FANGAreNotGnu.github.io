Applied Scientist @ AWS AutoGluon

I am an Applied Scientist based in Seattle, working on democratizing machine learning through automated systems. My research interests span Reinforcement Learning, Agentic Coding, and LLM Post-training.

My primary research focuses on agentic LLM post-training, specifically building autonomous agents to automate and scale reinforcement learning workflows. I also develop frameworks for general end-to-end multimodal deep learning automation, such as AutoGluon Assistant (aka MLZero) .

Previously, I developed AutoGluon Multimodal from release 0.7 to 1.5 .
