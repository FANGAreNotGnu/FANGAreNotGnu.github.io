I am an Applied Scientist based in Seattle, working on democratizing machine learning through automated systems. My research interests span Reinforcement Learning, Agentic Coding, and LLM Post-training.

My primary research focuses on agentic LLM post-training, specifically utilizing autonomous agents to automate and scale reinforcement learning workflows. I also develop frameworks for general end-to-end machine learning automation, such as AutoGluon Assistant (aka MLZero).

I am also a core developer of AutoGluon Multimodal.
